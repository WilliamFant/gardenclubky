About Us

The Garden Club of Kentucky,Inc.

Organization

The Garden Club of Kentucky (GCKY) is a member of the National Garden Clubs and has approximately 1,200 members in five districts. The club has several elected and appointed officers who oversee various committees, including arboriculture, and youth programs.

Educational Opportunities

GCKY offers courses for national accreditation in flower show judging, landscape and environmental consulting, and gardening. Local clubs hold monthly programs on diverse topics.

Community Outreach

GCKY provides Merit Scholarships in fields related to horticulture, which are available to junior or senior university students. GCKY helps local clubs with their beautification and conservation projects.
